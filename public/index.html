What's For Lunch

This is an exploration of food habits of Londoners, depending on their income and their area of residency. The rationale behind the project is that different social classes have different consumption patterns, hence demand different types of retail. The project includes an interactive map for the visualisation of the spatial distribution of different words in the name of food premises across London.
