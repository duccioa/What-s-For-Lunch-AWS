Data Sources

Food Premises Data concerning food premises’ names and location come from the Food Standard Agency and can be found here. The data provide the food hygiene rating or inspection result given to a business and reflect the standards of food hygiene found on the date of inspection or visit by the local authority. Businesses include restaurants, pubs, cafés, takeaways, hotels and other places consumers eat, as well as supermarkets and other food shops.

 

Household Median Income levels are obtained from the London Data Store and refer to the estimate of the year 2012-2013.

 

Wards and Borough boundaries are downloaded from the London Data Store.

 

The underlying map of London is provided by Mapbox.
