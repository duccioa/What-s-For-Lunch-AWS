Waitrose

Metrics for the analysis of the location of Waitrose stores show concentration along the north-west corridor through Hampstead and Finchley, the inner west corridor through Westminster, Chelsea and Hammersmith and also the south-east corridor through Clapham and Wimbledon.

There is a strong positive correlation between the relative concentration (location quotient) of Waitrose stores and median ward income suggesting that Waitrose targets the preferences of London’s higher socio-economic residents.
